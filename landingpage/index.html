Why choose our server?

Because we were also players and we know the usual problems in this game. For this reason, we have learned from the mistakes of others and we know that we can offer our players quality and a good game.

  • The server will be long lasting.
  • All players will be given equal rights.
  • The game team is made up of experienced people.

At the same time, we try to build a community of beautiful and well-behaved people.
So we are happy to invite you to be a part of it because we know that you will bring a plus to the community!

The experience rate will be 200%, the Yang rate will be 150% and the Object Drop rate will be 150%.

During the weekend, from Friday morning to Sunday evening, all rates will be increased by 100%.

It is crucial to complete all the quests (both PvM and PvP) because they will help you a lot in the evolution of your character, but at the same time they will keep the boredom away.

All these quests are designed by us from scratch, therefore they are unique. They deserve to be completed!

Items will require time and farming to be able to evolve to an advanced level.

The bonuses will be relatively accessible to everyone, but only the ambitious will achieve perfectly bonus items.

All monsters, even those in the first village (map1) will drop items. Farming at various levels will benefit everyone.

For example, the drop of certain upgrade items will be strictly available to monsters and low-level dungeons that are not so popular: Monkey Dungeon, Ork Wizards, Monsters from the Land of Fire and so on.
Various farming maps (with Metin Stones and various Bosses) will also be available for PvM lovers.

PvM Events will usually be organized on a predefined map, and these will be either boss events or metin stones events.

PvP Events will be organized in a pre-selected map, a kind of arena, and a GM will make sure that everything goes according to plan.
Special Events will be organized on a special map, but this may change for certain events such as Hide & Seek. This category also includes the well-known OX Event.

The systems present in the game will be the classic ones (mounts, pets, switchbot and so on).

We don't want to overload the gameplay with useless systems, which confuses the player more than helping him.
